# Super Xenon Galaxy
In Super Xenon Galaxy you match your color with the related energy to pick them up. For each energy you collect you will get a small speed boost which will stack the more you get.

There are enemies which will try to stop you, breaking your momentum and streak. But don't worry. There's also a powerup which will allow you to collect all the energy of your current color when you pick it up.

 The game is intended to be played with the custom made controller. But is also playable with a keyboard.
